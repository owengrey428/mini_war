ARSENAL GLOSSARY

Explosives & Guns

  • Shotgun: Close-range 3-pellet burst.
  • Bazooka: Massive cratering explosion.
  • Grenade: Arcing explosive with terrain damage.
  • Minigun: Rapid-fire stream of bullets.
  • Spreadfire: Short-range cone of flame pellets.
  • Homing Missile: Seeks nearest enemy automatically.
  • Cluster Bomb: Air-bursts into 5 bomblets.

Magic & Powers

  • Voodoo: Reflects all damage taken for 10s.
  • Cloaker: Turn into a single pixel to hide.
  • Freezer: Stops enemies mid-air in a 25px radius.
  • Teleport: Safe-drop to a random surface.
  • Steal: Randomly rips 3 items from enemy inventory.
  • Heal: Restores a chunk of lost health.
  • Mega Speed: Doubles movement speed temporarily.
  • Mega Jump: Doubles jump height temporarily.

Tech & Events

  • Airstrike: 25 bombs blanket the enemy's half.
  • Lightning: Seeks out players not under cover.
  • Boulder: Rolling rock that crushes everything.
  • Missile Tower: Auto-turret that guards an area.
  • Plasma Wall: Creeping shield that dissolves over 6s.
  • Superbridge: Constructs a solid platform bridge.
  • Rear Turret: Backward-firing auto cannon.
  • Mine: Invisible pressure-triggered explosive.
  • Fake Crate: Decoy crate that explodes on pickup.

Biology

  • Toxic Gas: Persistent 40-damage poison cloud.
  • Sheep: Wanders around. Kill for +25 HP.
  • Ants: Tiny explosive homing drones.
  • Holotrooper: A decoy that fires fake lasers.
  • Ghost: Ethereal entity that haunts enemies.
  • Boomerang: Returns after hitting terrain or foe.
  • Laser: Instant screen-wide raycast burn.
